Cinnamon rolls

Cinnamon Rolls are a type of pastry where the dough is flattened out. slathered with cinnamon filling, and then rolled up and cut into little cinnamon swirls. Top with icing and its the best thing ever. join the cinnaroll fan club today. The best parts of the roll?

  1. Cinnamon
  2. Cream Cheese Icing
  3. Gooey center
  4. Golden brown color
